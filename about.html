ABOUT

My name is Kendall Caison. I grew up in a small beach town in North Carolina. I started surfing when I was four years old and competed along the east coast for several years. I suffered a traumatic injury to my chest, resulting in reconstruction surgery. This marked the end of my competitive surfing career. Since I have surfed strictly for hobby, I turned to guitar in search of something do to in the winter months.
