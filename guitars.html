GUITARS

 As inspired by my influences, I currently own a Fender Stratocaster and a 50’s Standard Gibson Les Paul. My Fender is a Jimi Hendrix model, a special edition that incorporates a reverse neck and headstock to resemble Jimi’s left-handed style. This guitar sparked my motivation to learn and play more than ever. My Les Paul is a Gold-Top edition, an almost exact model played by Dickey Betts. This model took to revive 50’s Gibson characteristics including the vintage tuners, that really caught my attention. In addition to my electric guitars, I own a Big Baby Taylor acoustic guitar.
